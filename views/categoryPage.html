Fruits

In botany, a fruit is the seed-bearing structure in flowering plants that is formed from the ovary after flowering.

Nuts

A nut is a fruit consisting of a hard or tough nutshell protecting a kernel which is usually edible.

Exotic

Exotic fruits are that which are not native and that are cultivated outside, available at their place of origin.

Fresh

Fresh produce means fruits and vegetables that have not been processed in any manner.
